# Templates

Templates are **NOT** terminal prompts. Do **not** load them with `@` in your IDE.

Templates are **copy-paste documents**. You copy the content, adapt the placeholders to your session context, and paste it into your chat session.

---

## Available Templates

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| `sprint-testing-prompt.md` | Orchestrate multi-ticket sprint testing with auto-progression |

---

## How to Use

1. Open the template file.
2. Copy everything between the `--- START COPY ---` and `--- END COPY ---` markers.
3. Edit the **NOTES** section: replace placeholders with your session-specific context (previous ticket results, remaining tickets, observations).
4. Paste the edited content into your chat session.

---

## Why Templates Instead of Prompts?

Prompts (`@` loaded) are static instructions the AI follows as-is.
Templates require human input before use -- you fill in session notes, ticket queues, and context that changes every sprint.
